# **DESIGN GUIDELINES**

## **Color Palette**

**Primary Colors:**

* \*\*Royal Gold:\*\* \#D4AF37 (headers, accents, PK-related content, CTAs)  
* \*\*Deep Green:\*\* \#1B4D3E (traditional, represents ridge/nature, secondary buttons)  
* \*\*Kente Red:\*\* \#8B0000 (important announcements, urgent CTAs)

**Secondary Colors:**

* \*\*White:\*\* \#FFFFFF (clean backgrounds)  
* \*\*Warm Gray:\*\* \#F5F5F0 (section backgrounds, alternating sections)  
* \*\*Charcoal:\*\* \#2C3E50 (body text, paragraphs)  
* \*\*Light Gold:\*\* \#FFF8DC (subtle highlights, hover states)

**Accent Colors:**

* \*\*Heritage Blue:\*\* \#1E4D8B (links, informational buttons)  
* \*\*Earth Brown:\*\* \#8B4513 (sub-sections, borders)

## **Typography**

**Headings:**

* \*\*Font Family:\*\* "Playfair Display" or "Merriweather"  
* Style: Elegant, dignified, traditional feel  
* Sizes:  
* H1: 48px (desktop), 36px (mobile)  
* H2: 36px (desktop), 28px (mobile)  
* H3: 28px (desktop), 24px (mobile)  
* H4: 24px (desktop), 20px (mobile)

**Body Text:**

* \*\*Font Family:\*\* "Inter" or "Open Sans"  
* Style: Clean, highly readable, modern  
* Size: 16-18px (important for older diaspora readers)  
* Line Height: 1.6-1.8 (easy reading)

**Special/Accent:**

* \*\*Font Family:\*\* "Cinzel"  
* Use: His Majesty's name, special titles (use sparingly)

## **Responsive Design Requirements**

**Mobile-First Considerations:**

* Large tap targets (minimum 44x44px for all buttons)  
* Readable font sizes (minimum 16px body text)  
* Easy-to-use hamburger menu  
* Fast loading images (WebP format, lazy loading)  
* Sticky header with subscribe button always visible  
* Click-to-call phone numbers  
* WhatsApp integration option

**Breakpoints:**

* Mobile: 320px \- 767px  
* Tablet: 768px \- 1024px  
* Desktop: 1025px+
